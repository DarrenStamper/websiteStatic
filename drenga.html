Let's Spice It Up

Drenga

Drenga is a drinking game that involves a jenga tower, with each piece corresponding to a drinking rule. Each player must take it in turn to remove a piece from the tower, perform the rule corresponding to the piece, then place it on top of the tower.

What do you need?

  • Plain or numbered Jenga set [see settings]
  • Alcohol
  • King cup
How to play?

Ensure the screen can be viewed by everyone, and select someone to be responsible for entering the numbers into the site.

Build the tower.

Take a piece from the tower [can't be from the top 3 rows], enter the rule number, perform the rule, then place the piece on top of the tower. Repeat moving around the circle.

Whoever knocks the tower over must drink the king cup!
