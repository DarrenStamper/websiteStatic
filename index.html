Home on the net

Welcome to my little corner of the internet, made primarily for myself. This is my creative refuge I like to escape to from time to time, where I try to capture the magic of the old internet. Here you will find no adverts, no big tech, just hand crafted self expression. It's comfortable here, away from the noise of the world, getting lost in lines of code and dreamy 90s pixel art. The mind goes quiet...

Are you tired of social media, do you wish to seek refuge from a world of monitization and doom scrolling? then take a stroll off the beaten path, delve deeper into the indie web. Feel free to look around, or if you'd like to go down the rabbit hole, click on one of the button links above, and you shall be taken to one of the many personal websites I have stumbled upon in my own adventures through cyberspace. Many such websites will have their own links to more websites, and before long you will be surfing the net like its 1999!
